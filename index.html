Our Natural Treasures

Three essential elements of our ecosystem that need protection

Majestic Mountains

Mountains are the Earth's natural water towers, providing freshwater to over half of humanity. They are home to 15% of the world's population and host about half of the world's biodiversity hotspots.

Climate change and human activities threaten these fragile ecosystems, leading to glacier melt and habitat loss.

Flowing Rivers

Rivers are the lifeblood of our planet, providing water for drinking, agriculture, and industry. They support diverse ecosystems and have shaped human civilizations for millennia.

Pollution, damming, and over-extraction threaten these vital waterways, putting both human and animal communities at risk.

Ancient Trees

Ancient trees are living monuments that have witnessed centuries of history. These centenarian giants are biodiversity hotspots, providing habitat for countless species and storing massive amounts of carbon.

Deforestation, climate change, and disease threaten these ancient beings, with many species on the brink of extinction.
